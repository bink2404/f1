Joining the game

You must first create a user i.d. that you will use to log into the game.


Creating teams

You can create up to 3 teams per user i.d.
Each team is made up from 3 drivers and 1 constructor.


Creating leagues

You can create a private league for you and your friends to join. Once created, you are given a league i.d. number, which you use along with the password when joining the league.


Joining leagues

Each of your teams can be a member of either a public or private league. Public leagues are divided according to player skill level, and you can join any one you want.
However, even if a team is not a member of a league, it will still earn points.


Changing teams

You can change your team up to the deadline for the race, which is set at midnight GMT on the Saturday morning before each race day.
Each driver and constructor can only be chosen a maximum of 5 times per season.


Prizes

There are no prizes available, apart from the bragging rights if you win.
